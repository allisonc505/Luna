A community solution to improve social connection among full-time shift time workers

What
Luna is a friend-making/dating app for people who work different hours and are forced to have different sleep-wake cycles than normal people. Users on the app can connect with people who have similar schedule as them, browse and join events, or get tips on adjusting their cycle for certain social occasions to stay socially active.

Process
I designed the the product through the following phases: Research, Emphasize, Define, Prototype, and Evaluation. I moved back and forth among the five phases and redefine our project scope while learning more about our problem statement.
Problem Context
Discover
I identified interview subjects with either medical knowledge or who had participate in some forms of shift work. Expert interviews revealed a deeper concern for shift time workers' social isolation, which can be overlooked by users when they have no choice to prioritize their need to avoid excessive sleepiness and wake-time fatigue. As for the end-users, while most of them pull through their schedules by turning to medication, restriction on alcohol and drugs, or night routines, some still find social life the hardest to manage and believe that "the only real way to maintain normal relationships is when I can switch to sleeping at nights."
Among different types of shift time workers, we decided to focus on full-time shift time workers who work stable night/evening shifts and not one day during the week could they squeeze time to meet with their friends and family.
Define Challenge
How Might We help full-time shift time workers avoid social isolation and improve their mental health through staying socially active?

According to statements by Luciana Torquati, lead author of the study and a researcher at the University of Exeter in the UK, I found that it's of crucial importance to better user's mental health. Prototype
Luna is a friend-making/dating app for people who work different hours and are forced to have different sleep-wake cycles than normal people. Users on the app can connect with people who have similar schedule as them, browse and join events, or get tips on adjusting their cycle for certain social occasions to stay socially active.
